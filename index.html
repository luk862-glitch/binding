Style and Content

This curation examines the false divide between style and content, showing how style shapes culture, signals identity, and communicates values.

Colophon

This project was designed and coded by Katie Lu. It is typeset in Clash Grotesk. This collection includes Jeffrey Keedy’s Style Is Not a Four Letter Word, Susan Sontag’s On Style, and Andrew Blauvelt’s Under the Surface of Style. This work was produced in New York in 2025.
